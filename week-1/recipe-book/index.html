Curries

A plate of plain white (or flavored) rice portioned evenly with sauce filled with chunks of meat. The dish varies in spicy-ness depending on the tolerancy of the one eating the dish, or where its made.

Omelets

Also known as omurice, a semi-popular Japanese dish that is basically composed of rice that is cooked inside a French omelet. It can be a little difficult to make at first, but it is very tasty.

Soups

Miso soup is made with dashi, a stock made of kelp, bonito flakes, and fermented soybean paste known as miso. The dashi softens the miso into a thin liquid.
